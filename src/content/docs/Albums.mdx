Album entries are for collections of songs in a form of physical or digital albums.

These entries include album-related metadata (as _fields_) such as names, catalog numbers, crossfade links and _connections_ to other entry types such as artists, songs and events.

A full list of album-related rules is available [here](/rules/albums)
