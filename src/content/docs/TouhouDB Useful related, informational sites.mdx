This page lists official and unofficial Touhou-related websites, mostly encyclopedias, archives, and other informational sites. Many of these sites are commonly used as references on TouhouDB.

Commonly used music related stores and commercial websites can also be found here.

See our [partner websites](/docs/documentation/vocadb-partner-websites).
