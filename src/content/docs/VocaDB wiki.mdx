Welcome to the [VocaDB](http://vocadb.net/) wiki!

VocaDB is a collaborative database for [Vocaloid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vocaloid), UTAU and other singing synthesizers, with artists, discography, PVs and more. Our goal is to be the most accurate and complete source of Vocaloid discography and artists.

For more information about VocaDB, see [the complete description](/docs/documentation/vocadb-what-is-it-for) and [history](/docs/documentation/vocadbs-history).

This wiki is primarily used for documenting guidelines and rules for the site.
