When adding an album, include a verifiable source or reference confirming the existence of the album. This includes, but not limited to:

- The link to the album on streaming services (YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, Bandcamp, etc.)
- The link to the album store page (Amazon, iTunes, Booth, Bandcamp, etc.)
- The link to the album on the artist's website, blog, or social media profiles
- The crossfade/full album stream on the artist's channels (added on the "Media" tab; as a PV)
- A description that includes relevant, verifiable information
