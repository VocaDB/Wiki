Loanwords are romanized into their original language.

## Examples

For "[Romeo and Cinderella](https://vocadb.net/S/1032)",

- "ロミオ" would be romanized as "Romeo".
- "シンデレラ" would be romanized as "Cinderella".

For "[Two-Sided Lovers](https://vocadb.net/S/1508)", "ラバーズ" would be romanized as "lovers".

For "[Brain Fluid Explosion Girl](https://vocadb.net/S/17189)", "ガール" would be romanized as "girl".
