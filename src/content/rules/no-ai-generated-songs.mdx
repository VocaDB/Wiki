Song entries for AI-generated songs (AI-generated music or AI-generated uncontrolled vocal synthesis) are not allowed. For example, songs using one or more of these tools are not allowed.

- [Suno](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suno_(platform)>)
- [Udio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Udio)
- [Riffusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Riffusion)
- [AIVA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIVA)
- [YuE](https://map-yue.github.io/)
- [Eleven Music](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ElevenLabs#Products)
- [Lyria (from Google)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_DeepMind#Music_generation)
