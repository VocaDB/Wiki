It's common to see artists credited with additional honorifics, especially in Japanese (e.g. "-san"/"さん", "-sama"/"様"). 

These honorifics should not be added, unless the artist uses those themselves, for example:

- [外道さん (Gedou-san)](https://vocadb.net/Ar/1823)
