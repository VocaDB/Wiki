Songs without voice-synth vocals (vocaloid/utau/cevio/etc.) should not be added, unless:
- The song is part of an album entry.
- The song has at least 5 voice-synth covers (these can be added as [cover unifier entries](https://vocadb.net/T/6751/out-of-scope-cover-unifier)).
