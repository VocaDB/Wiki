If an original PV is unavailable (deleted, hidden by the author, or blocked worldwide), do not remove the link. Instead, mark the PV as unavailable.

You can check the availability using the [YouTube region restriction checker](https://polsy.org.uk/stuff/ytrestrict.cgi).
